## Success Factors for Data Lakes

1. **Right Platform** - Scalable, cost-effective, handles variety
2. **Right Data** - Save raw data, break silos
3. **Right Interface** - Self-service, searchable, role-appropriate

## Common Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Result | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Just store data | Data swamp | Organize, catalog |
| Encrypt everything | Can't find anything | Selective, on-demand |
| No governance | Chaos | Zones, policies |
| IT controls all | Bottleneck | Self-service |

## Essential Capabilities

- **Zones** - Different governance levels
- **Catalog** - Find and understand data
- **Tag-based policies** - Scalable governance
- **Self-service access** - Request-based
- **Automation** - Scale beyond manual

## The Future: Data Ocean

- Catalog all enterprise data
- Access in place, don't copy
- Govern centrally
- Comply with regulations
